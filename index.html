Welcome to Bahay ni Rob!

Experience the warmth of home-cooked Filipino meals in every bite. At Bahay ni Rob, we specialize in authentic comfort food that brings nostalgia and joy to every Filipino table.

Founded in 2020 by a family of food lovers from Bacoor, Cavite. Bahay ni Rob started as a small karinderya and has grown into a cozy cafe offering signature dishes loved by many. What sets us apart? Our dishes are made with fresh, local ingredients, cooked with love, and served with a smile.

⭐ Best Sellers: Kare-Kare, Crispy Pata, and our creamy Halo-Halo!
